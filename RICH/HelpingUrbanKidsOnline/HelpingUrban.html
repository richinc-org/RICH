About Author

Jacqueline O. Dawson, Ph.D., is the co-founder and executive director of the Reach Into Cultural Heights (RICH) organization. A non-profit with more than three decades committed to guiding urban young people to experience becoming positive learners. A visionary leader, Jacqueline creates innovative self-directed learning conversations designed for the urban student. Her passion is watching the transformation of the hesitant learner become a confident and competent self-directed academic achiever.
