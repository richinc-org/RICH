    Cultural Diversity Project


    The RICH organization has chosen the Peace Run website to introduce to you to a creative website that is bringing a peaceful message to young people from around the world. RICH is presenting the Peace Run website for you to joyfully experience learning about cultures other than your own. Our vision for you is to become a thoughtful teenager, respectful to others who are different from you, and to become an adult who will contribute to spreading peace throughout the world.

    Go to the Peace Run Discovery and choose a country. Why did you choose this country? Tell us what you learned about this country's cultures and traditions. How would you help this country spread peace?

    Elder Interview Project


    Choose an elder that is in your community and interview them about their life. What have they learned about being responsabile, considerate, using strategies, and how did they learn they matter? You can also ask other questions as well such as what are some life experiences they have learned and what do they miss the most about their youth. Remember to state which elder you chose?
